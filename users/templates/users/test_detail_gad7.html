GAD-7 Interpretation

The GAD-7 is a screening tool for Generalized Anxiety Disorder. It measures the severity of anxiety symptoms over the past two weeks.

Score Range Anxiety Severity Recommended Action
0-4 Minimal anxiety Monitor; may not require treatment
5-9 Mild anxiety Watchful waiting; consider follow-up
10-14 Moderate anxiety Possible clinically significant condition; consider intervention
15-21 Severe anxiety Active treatment; consider pharmacotherapy and/or psychotherapy
